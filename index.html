About the Artist

My Background

As a practicing attorney in New York City for 50 years, I had little time to explore my creative side. Now, as I transition to retirement, I have found immense pleasure in taking art classes and putting brush and palette knife to canvas to experience the utter joy of painting.

My Inspiration

I am drawn (pun intended) to the symphony of color, texture and movement that I see in nature and the emotions that they engender. By painting abstractly what I experience immersed in nature directly or by memory, and often guided by the sound of classical music playing in my studio, I enter a different state of consciousness, totally absorbed and energized by this creative process. It is my hope that the viewers of my art will find their own unique, emotional connection to the work.
